Ian's Chicken Breast

This is a chicken breast recipe that my mother taught me. I eat it 5 times a week lol.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Ground Cumin

Steps

  1. Thaw Chicken Breast
  2. Preheat Oven to 400F
  3. Season with listed seasonings
  4. Heat on cooking tray in oven for 30 minutes
